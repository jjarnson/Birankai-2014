Catalog

Proceeds from the sale of DVDs support scholarship within Birankai North America. Scholarship grants help teachers to attend Summer Camp and defray a portion of the costs to bring Chiba Sensei to regional events. Grants also support development seminars for small, young or struggling dojos and fund work study programs at Summer Camp.

This collection of DVDs covers Birankai Summer Camps 2004 through 2009. Aikido techniques are demonstrated by Birankai and guest instructors, including M. Ueshiba Doshu, Yamada Shihan, T.K. Chiba Shihan, Miyamoto Shihan, and many others. A table of contents for each DVD has been provided.
